Who we are

Majime is a community of streamer friends that enjoys streaming on twitch.tv every sort of thing! Come and discover every single streamer of the #Majime! With special collaborations, seasonal events and many other thing.

We want to provide you with the best entertainment we can possibly offer such as Video-gameplay,Podcasts, Just Chatting, Art. Even if some of us may not be really good at gaming,but hey, we still like to try our best even tho we’re probably end up failing, badly. But it won’t matter if in the end we managed to bring a smile onto your face!

Our goal is to create a inclusive community where everyone can express themself as they wish and to feel at their comfort.
